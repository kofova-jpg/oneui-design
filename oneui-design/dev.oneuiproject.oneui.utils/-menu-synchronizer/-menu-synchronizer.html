Parameters

bottomNavView

The BottomNavigationView to synchronize the menu items with.

toolbar

The Toolbar to synchronize the menu items with.

onMenuItemClick

A callback to handle menu item click events.

initialState

(Optional) The initial state of the menu - either State.PORTRAIT, State.LANDSCAPE, or State.HIDDEN.

maxActionItems

(Optional) The maximum number of items to show as action items in the Toolbar. By default, it's 2.

copyIcon

(Optional) Boolean flag to determine whether to show icons on the Toolbar menu items. By default, it's true when there are more than one item in the menu and maxActionItems is greater than 1.
